    Our History

    Hilltop Farms Equestrian Center is a full-service boarding facility located on the Southwest side of Indianapolis in West Newton, Indiana. Our farm has been a part of the community in West Newton, Indiana for over 120 years. Members of our team have been training and showing horses for over 30 years.
